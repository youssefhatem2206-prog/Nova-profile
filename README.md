## 📂 Projects  
Here is a brief overview of the most significant projects included in my portfolio:

### 1. 🎵 Spotify Data Visualization  
**Description:**  
A Streamlit app visualizing Spotify data, including track counts, top playlists, and artist follower statistics.  

**Tools & Technologies:**  
- Python, Pandas, Plotly, Streamlit  
- Data exploration and interactive visualizations  

🔗 [View Project](https://github.com/youssef13494/Spotify_Dashboard)

---

### 2. 📊 Bank Customer Churn Prediction  
**Description:**  
A machine learning model that predicts if a customer will leave the bank using factors like credit score, balance, and age.  

**Tools & Technologies:**  
- Scikit-Learn, Pandas, Gradio  
- Deployed on Hugging Face Spaces  

🔗 [View on Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co/spaces/Youssef-Hatem/Banking-churn)  

---

### 3. 📈 Sales Forecasting and Optimization  
**Description:**  
A project that forecasts sales using ARIMA models and deploys them via Streamlit. The app helps businesses optimize their sales strategies.  

**Tools & Technologies:**  
- ARIMA, Python, Streamlit, MLOps  
- Time-series forecasting  

---

### 4. 🏥 Skin Disease Diagnosis System  
**Description:**  
Award-winning graduation project that utilizes **deep learning** to diagnose skin diseases accurately. Achieved 2nd place in the Sixth Computer Science Olympiad.  

**Tools & Technologies:**  
- Neural Networks, Deep Learning
